Policies

Refund Policy

Refunds will be issued only for cancellations made within 7 days of purchase for the full monthly price of $75. No refunds will be provided after this period due to the nature of the service and access to exclusive content. Refund requests must be submitted via email with proof of purchase.

Cancellation Policy

Subscribers can cancel their membership anytime through account settings or by contacting support. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds will be provided for mid-cycle cancellations.

Expectations Before and After Purchase

Before Purchase:

  • Ensure a basic understanding of stock trading for full benefit.
  • Agree to comply with all community guidelines, including respectful communication and no sharing of proprietary content.
  • Understand this service is for educational purposes, not professional financial advice.

After Purchase:

  • Immediate access to the group chat and educational resources.
  • Active participation encouraged for the best learning experience.
  • Maintain confidentiality and avoid redistributing content.
  • Violations of group rules may result in suspension or termination without refund.

Delivery Policy

Access to the group chat will be granted within 24 hours of successful payment. Educational materials and additional resources will be shared via the group chat or designated platforms. If access is not granted within 24 hours, contact support at OnlyBullish1@gmail.com.
